h2.section-heading.text-uppercase.text-center Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2025

At Liz Graphy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This privacy policy explains how our photo editing software handles your data, including information about our AI features, third-party integrations, and local data storage practices.

1. Information We Collect and Store

Our application is primarily designed to process and store data locally on your device. We may collect and process the following types of data:

  • User-Provided Content : Images, photographs, and other media files that you upload, edit, or create using our software.
  • Application Usage Data : Information about how you use our software, including editing preferences, feature usage, and application performance metrics.
  • Account Information : If you create an account, we collect your email address and username to provide personalized services.
  • Technical Data : Device information, operating system version, and other technical information necessary for the software to function properly.
  • AI Processing Data : When you use our AI features (such as background generation or removal), we process image data to provide these services.

2. Local Data Storage

Our application primarily stores your data locally on your device:

  • The images and projects you create remain on your device and are not automatically uploaded to our servers.
  • Your editing history, preferences, and settings are stored locally.
  • You maintain control over your data, including the ability to delete it at any time.

3. Third-Party AI Services and APIs

Some advanced features in our application may utilize third-party AI services:

  • AI Image Processing : For features like background generation and removal, we may use third-party AI services.
  • Data Transmission : When using these features, specific image data may be temporarily transmitted to these third-party services for processing.
  • Data Retention : We ensure that third-party services do not permanently store your images unless explicitly stated and consented to.
  • Service Providers : We carefully select third-party providers that maintain high standards of data security and privacy.

We require all third-party service providers to respect the security of your data and to treat it in accordance with applicable laws.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our photo editing software and features
  • To process your images according to your editing instructions
  • To enable AI-powered features like background generation and removal
  • To maintain and optimize application performance
  • To provide technical support and respond to your inquiries
  • To send important updates about our software (with your consent)
  • To analyze usage patterns to improve our software (using anonymized data)

5. Your Image Rights and Intellectual Property

We respect your intellectual property rights:

  • You retain full ownership of the images you create, edit, or modify using our software.
  • We do not claim any ownership rights to your content.
  • We do not use your images for training our AI models unless you explicitly opt-in to such usage.
  • We do not share, sell, or distribute your images to third parties without your consent.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Local storage encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure transmission protocols when data is sent to third-party AI services
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Protection against unauthorized access or data breaches

7. Children's Privacy

Our software is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and export your data stored within our software
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of certain data processing activities
  • Object to how we process your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in our software and on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
